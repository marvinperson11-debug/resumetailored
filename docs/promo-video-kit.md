# ResumeTailored AI — Promo Video Kit (HeyGen + CapCut)

**Goal:** a punchy ~35-second vertical (9:16) promo for TikTok / Reels / Shorts.
**Ingredients you have:** your 6-min screen recording (720p), a HeyGen avatar, CapCut (free).
**The idea:** HeyGen avatar = the *voice/face*. Screen recording = the *proof*. CapCut = the *assembler*.

> You do **not** use the 6-min recording as-is. You mine it for ~5 short "money moments" (2–5s each), speed them up, and inter-cut them with the avatar.

---

## 1. HeyGen voiceover script (word-for-word)

Record this as **one HeyGen avatar clip** (or two: intro + outro). Keep energy high, conversational, ~150 words/min. Target ~33–36s total.

> **[HOOK — 0:00–0:04]**
> "I just tailored my resume to a job posting in under a minute. Watch how."
>
> **[PROBLEM — 0:04–0:08]**

**Alt hooks** (test 2–3, keep the winner):
- "POV: you tailor your resume to the job in 60 seconds instead of 6 hours."
- "This free AI writes a better resume than you paid ChatGPT to."
- "Recruiters can tell you didn't tailor your resume. Here's the 60-second fix."

**HeyGen tips:** pick a warm, upbeat avatar + voice; export **vertical 9:16** if HeyGen offers it, otherwise export and crop in CapCut; enable HeyGen captions OFF (you'll add cleaner ones in CapCut).

## 2. CapCut storyboard (shot-by-shot)

Canvas: **1080×1920 (9:16)**. Total ~35s. "Clip" = a trimmed piece of your screen recording.

| # | Time | Video on screen | Source | Speed | Motion / effect | On-screen caption |
|---|------|-----------------|--------|-------|-----------------|-------------------|
| 1 | 0:00–0:04 | HeyGen avatar, full frame | HeyGen | 1× | Slight zoom-in (Ken Burns) | **"Resume tailored in 60 seconds"** (big, top) |
| 2 | 0:04–0:08 | Avatar → cut to your desktop | HeyGen + rec | 1× | Hard cut on beat | "Stop sending generic resumes" |
| 3 | 0:08–0:12 | Pasting the **job URL** + hitting Tailor | recording | 2–3× | Punch-in zoom on the URL field, then the button | "Paste any job link 🔗" |

| 8 | 0:30–0:35 | Avatar returns, full frame | HeyGen | 1× | Zoom-in + logo lower-third | **"Free daily → resumetailored.com"** |

**Editing rules of thumb:**
- **Cut on the beat** of the background track — every caption change lands on a beat.
- **Punch-in zooms** (110–130%) on every click/button. This is what makes app demos feel "wild" instead of boring.
- **Speed ramps:** boring typing/loading = 3–4×; the reveal moments = 1×.
- Add a subtle **whoosh/click SFX** on each transition (CapCut has free ones).
- Keep every caption ≤ 5 words, big, high-contrast, centered-ish (safe zone: keep text out of the top 12% and bottom 15% so TikTok UI doesn't cover it).

## 3. The 16:9 → 9:16 conversion (important)

Your recording is **landscape (1280×720)**; the promo is **vertical**. Two options:

**Option A — Browser mockup frame (recommended, looks polished):**
1. New 1080×1920 project, background = a brand color or soft gradient (use your template's navy/accent).
2. Drop the landscape clip in the **upper-middle**, scaled to ~90% width, with rounded corners + a soft shadow (looks like a floating browser window).
3. Captions go in the space above/below the clip. Avatar can sit as a **circle** in a lower corner during the demo.

**Option B — Crop & follow (faster, but loses edges):**
1. Scale the landscape clip up to fill the vertical frame.
2. Keyframe the position so the frame "follows" the action (pan to the URL field, then to the resume). More work per clip.

Go with **A** unless you're in a hurry.
