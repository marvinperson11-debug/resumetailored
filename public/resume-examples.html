How to use a resume example the right way

An example shows you which keywords matter for a role — it is not a template to copy. ATS rank resumes by how closely they mirror the specific posting, so a generic sample copied word-for-word works against you. Read the example, note the skills it highlights, then rewrite your own real experience to match the exact job. That's what ResumeTailored does automatically — it never invents experience you don't have.
