How to use a resume example the right way

An example shows you which keywords matter for a role — it is not a template to copy. Applicant tracking systems rank resumes by how closely they mirror the specific job posting, so a generic sample copied word-for-word actually works against you. The winning move is to read the example, note the skills and terms it highlights, then rewrite your own real experience to match the exact job you are applying to. That is what ResumeTailored does automatically: it reads the posting, pulls the right keywords, and rewrites your bullets in the role's language — without ever inventing experience you don't have.
