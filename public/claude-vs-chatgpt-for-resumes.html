The practical differences

For resume work, writing quality and instruction-following matter most: natural phrasing that doesn't read as AI-generated, and faithful rewriting that never fabricates experience.

In our own product experience, Claude tends to produce restrained, professional phrasing and is careful about staying within the facts you give it — which is exactly what you want when reframing real experience for ATS.

ChatGPT is highly capable too; results vary by model and prompt. The point of a dedicated tool is that you don't have to engineer prompts — the tailoring logic is built in.
