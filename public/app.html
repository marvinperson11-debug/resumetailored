Salary Negotiation Guide

Know your worth. Use these strategies and resources to negotiate confidently and get paid what you deserve.

📊

Know Your Market Value

Before negotiating, research salary ranges for your exact role, location, and experience level. Never go in blind.

Levels.fyi → Glassdoor → LinkedIn Salary → BLS.gov (official) →
🎯

The Negotiation Framework

Follow this proven 5-step process for every offer:

  1. Never give a number first. Let them anchor.
  2. Get the full offer in writing before responding.
  3. Take 24–48 hours to "review" before countering.
  4. Counter 10–20% above your target number.
  5. Negotiate total comp — salary, equity, PTO, remote days, signing bonus.
💬

Scripts That Work

When they ask your number:
"Based on my research and experience, I'm targeting a range of $X to $Y. What budget have you allocated for this role?"
When making a counter offer:
"I'm very excited about this opportunity. Based on my [X years of experience / specific skills], I was hoping we could get closer to $Y. Is there flexibility there?"
When negotiating beyond salary:
"If the base salary is firm, I'd love to explore an additional week of PTO and a signing bonus to bridge the gap."
📈

After You Land the Role

Salary growth doesn't stop at the offer. Keep advancing:

  • Document every win and contribution from Day 1
  • Request a formal performance review at 6 months
  • Benchmark your salary annually — markets shift fast
  • Don't wait for a raise — ask for it proactively
  • Every new role is a chance to reset your salary ceiling
  • Build relationships with recruiters even when employed

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free tier work?▾
You get 1 free resume tailoring per day — no credit card required. Each day at midnight the counter resets. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited tailorings.
How do I verify my Pro subscription?▾
After subscribing, enter the email you used at checkout into the email field on the Resume Tailor tab and click away. Your Pro status will activate automatically.
Will the AI make up experience I don't have?▾
No. Our AI strictly rewrites and reframes your existing experience using the job posting's language and keywords. It never adds skills or experience you didn't include in your original resume.
How do I cancel my Pro subscription?▾
Send us a message using the form on this page with your email and we'll cancel immediately. No questions asked. You can also reply to any of our emails.
What format should I paste my resume in?▾
Plain text works best. Open your resume in Word or Google Docs, select all (Ctrl+A), copy (Ctrl+C), and paste into the resume field. The AI handles the formatting.
