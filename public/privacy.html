Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026  ·  Effective: June 9, 2026

Short version: We collect only what we need to run the service. We don't sell your data. You can delete your account and data at any time by emailing support@resumetailored.com.

1. Who We Are

ResumeTailored AI ("we," "us," or "our") operates resumetailored.com, an AI-powered resume tailoring and career tools platform. Our contact email is support@resumetailored.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated automatically when you use the service.

Information you provide:

  • Account information: email address, username, and password (hashed)
  • Resume content and job descriptions you paste or upload
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe — we never store raw card numbers)
  • Support messages you send us
  • Career check-in and community forum posts

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address (used for free-tier usage tracking, not stored long-term)
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent (via Google Analytics, subject to your cookie consent)
  • Session tokens stored in your browser's localStorage

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the service
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To send transactional emails (password resets, subscription confirmations)
  • To enforce free-tier usage limits
  • To analyze aggregate usage patterns and improve features
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or resume content with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential cookies: Session authentication (always active)
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics 4 — only activated after you accept cookies via our consent banner
  • Advertising cookies: Google Ads — only activated after consent

You can withdraw cookie consent at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site, or by clicking "Reject" if the banner reappears.

We implement Google Consent Mode v2, which means analytics and ad tracking are disabled by default until you give explicit consent.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only with the following service providers, under data processing agreements:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — processes your resume text to generate AI outputs. Anthropic's API is used under their standard enterprise terms.
  • Stripe — processes payments. Stripe is PCI-DSS certified.
  • Resend — sends transactional emails (if configured)
  • Google Analytics — usage analytics (consent-gated)
  • Railway — cloud hosting provider

6. Data Retention

Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. In-memory data (usage counters, sessions) resets on server restart. Resume content sent to the AI is not stored after the response is returned.

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, email support@resumetailored.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent for cookies at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@resumetailored.com.

8. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. International Transfers

Our service is hosted in the United States via Railway. If you access it from the EU, EEA, or UK, your data may be transferred to the US. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses where required.

10. Security

We use HTTPS for all data in transit. Passwords are hashed before storage. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@resumetailored.com.
