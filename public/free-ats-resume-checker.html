What Is an ATS Resume Checker?

Why 75% of Resumes Never Reach a Human

An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is software that companies use to automatically screen job applications before a recruiter ever sees them. When you submit a resume online, an ATS reads it, scores it against the job description, and typically discards any resume that doesn't hit a minimum match threshold.

ATS systems score your resume based on keyword matches — does your resume contain the specific skills, job titles, tools, and phrases mentioned in the job posting? If your resume uses different terminology (e.g., "customer success" when the job says "account management"), an ATS may score it as a mismatch even if you're perfectly qualified.

An ATS resume checker helps you understand your current match score and identify what's missing. But most checkers stop there — they show you the problem and leave you to fix it. ResumeTailor AI shows you the score and automatically rewrites your resume to improve it.
