How we think about "best"

"Best" depends on the job you're hiring the tool for. Building a resume once, tracking applications, scoring keywords, and tailoring to each posting are different problems. Below, each tool is rated for what it genuinely does well — including where competitors beat us.

The 2026 shortlist

1. ResumeTailored AI

Best for tailoring to a specific job

$19/mo · free tier

Built around one job: rewriting your existing resume to match each job description using Anthropic's Claude, then writing a matching cover letter. Includes a job-URL importer, 100+ ATS-ready templates, PDF/Word export and a bilingual English/Chinese interface. One full tailoring free per day, no card.

2. Teal

Best free job-tracker

Free · AI features ~$29/mo

Strong free job-application tracker and Chrome extension. Its AI resume features and unlimited use sit behind a higher-priced subscription, and it does not generate cover letters on the free tier.

3. Jobscan

Best keyword scorer

$49.95/mo (~$30/mo annual)

Excellent at scoring how well your resume matches a job description and flagging missing keywords. It tells you what's missing — but it scores rather than rewrites your resume for you.

4. Rezi

Solid ATS-focused builder

$29/mo or ~$149 lifetime

A capable ATS-oriented resume builder with AI writing features. GPT-based; many users find its cover-letter output less natural than dedicated tailoring tools.

5. Kickresume

Best template variety

from ~$4.50/mo

Large library of designed templates and a low entry price. Some two-column template designs can reduce ATS parse reliability, so test exports against a scanner.

6. Enhancv

Best for visual design

~$25/mo

Design-forward templates and content tips. Great if polished visuals are your priority; tailoring to each specific job is not its core focus.

Why tailoring beats building

Most builders help you format one resume. But keyword-based ATS screening rewards a resume that matches each posting. That's why ResumeTailored focuses on per-job tailoring with Anthropic's Claude: paste your resume and a job posting, and get a rewritten, ATS-aligned resume plus a matching cover letter in about 30 seconds — while keeping every claim truthful.
