The Process

From generic to job-ready in 30 seconds

1
Step 1

Paste your current resume

Open your resume in Word, Google Docs, or any editor. Select all, copy, and paste the plain text into ResumeTailored. No formatting required — our AI reads the raw text and understands your full work history, skills, and experience level automatically.

2
Step 2

Paste the job posting

Copy the full job description from LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, or any company careers page. Paste the whole thing. The more detail you give our AI, the more precisely it can match your resume to what the hiring manager actually wants.

3
Step 3

AI rewrites your resume to match

Our AI — powered by Anthropic's Claude — reads both documents simultaneously. It identifies which of your experiences are most relevant to this specific role, rewrites your bullet points using the job posting's exact keywords, reorders sections so your most relevant work appears first, and rewrites your summary to speak directly to this company and role.

4
Step 4 — Optional

Get a custom cover letter too

Choose "Cover Letter" or "Both" mode and we'll also write a 3–4 paragraph cover letter tailored to this specific job. It opens with a compelling hook about the company, connects 2–3 of your strongest achievements to the role's requirements, and closes with a confident call to action. No generic filler.

5
Step 5

Copy, paste, and submit

One click copies your tailored resume and cover letter to your clipboard. Paste into your application. Submit. The whole process takes under 30 seconds — and you get an application that looks like you spent hours customizing it.
