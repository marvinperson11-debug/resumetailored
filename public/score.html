How does the free ATS resume score checker work?

Paste your resume (or upload a PDF or DOCX file) and paste a job description — or enter a job board URL. The AI calculates a keyword match score from 0 to 100%, identifies which required keywords are missing, reviews your resume's readability, and returns 5 specific AI-written rewrite suggestions. No account required.

What file formats does the resume checker accept?

Upload your resume as a PDF (.pdf) or Word document (.docx), or paste the text directly. All three input methods produce the same quality ATS keyword analysis.

Does this tool support Chinese resumes?

Yes. The AI translation feature converts your resume from Chinese (or any language) into professional English before running the ATS analysis — so non-native English speakers get accurate results. The interface is also available in Simplified Chinese.
