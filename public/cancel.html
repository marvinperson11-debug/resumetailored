Cancellation FAQs

Do I get a refund if I cancel mid-month?+
We don't offer prorated refunds for partial months, but you keep full Pro access through the end of your current billing period. If you were accidentally charged and contact us within 7 days, we'll issue a full refund, no questions asked.
How fast does cancellation take effect?+
We process all cancellation requests within 1 hour and send a confirmation email. Your Pro access continues until your current billing period ends — you're not cut off immediately.
Can I pause my subscription instead of canceling?+
We don't currently offer a pause feature, but canceling and re-subscribing later is just as simple. We never price-lock or penalize returning members — you'll always get the same $19/month rate when you come back.
What if I was charged but didn't mean to subscribe?+
Contact us within 7 days of the charge using the form above or at support@resumetailored.com and we'll issue a full refund immediately.
Will my data be deleted when I cancel?+
No. Your career check-in data, community posts, and account information stay intact after cancellation. If you'd like your data deleted, just request it and we'll handle it within 48 hours.
Is there a cancellation fee or early termination penalty?+
Absolutely not. There are zero cancellation fees, zero penalties, and zero strings attached. Month-to-month means exactly that — you're never locked in for longer than one month at a time.
Can I resubscribe after canceling?+
Yes, anytime. Just go to the app, enter your email, and click Upgrade. You'll get the same $19/month rate — we don't charge more for returning subscribers.
