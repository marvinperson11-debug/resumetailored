Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is it really free to start? +
Yes — the free tier gives you 1 AI resume tailoring per day with no credit card required. You can sign up and get your first tailored resume in under 2 minutes.
Will it fabricate experience I don't have? +
Never. Our AI strictly rewrites and reframes your existing experience using the job's language — it never adds skills or experience you don't have. You stay 100% truthful while communicating your real value more clearly.
What AI model does it use? +
We use Anthropic's Claude — the same AI trusted by Fortune 500 companies. It's significantly better at nuanced writing than older or cheaper models, which is why the output actually sounds like a talented human wrote it.
How is this different from Jobscan or Resume.io? +
Jobscan scores your resume and tells you what keywords to add — you still have to do the writing. Resume.io is a resume builder with templates. ResumeTailored AI actually rewrites your resume for you, using the exact language of the job posting, in 30 seconds.
Can I cancel Pro anytime? +
Yes, completely. Month-to-month, no contracts, no cancellation fees. You keep full Pro access through the end of your billing period when you cancel. Use the cancel page or email us and we'll handle it within 1 hour.
What formats can I export my resume in? +
Your tailored resume is available to copy as plain text (perfect for pasting into ATS forms) or download as a formatted .docx Word document. Both are included in free and Pro plans.
