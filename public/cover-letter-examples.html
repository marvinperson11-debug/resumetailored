What every strong cover letter has in common

Whatever the role or level, the letters that get read share the same bones: a first line that proves you can do this job, one or two achievements with specifics that mirror the posting, a sentence on why this employer, and a confident close — in about 250-350 words. ResumeTailored builds all of that from your real resume and the actual job description.
