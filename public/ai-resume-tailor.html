What to Look For

5 Criteria for a Great AI Resume Tailor

Not all AI resume tools are equal. Here's what separates a genuinely useful AI tailor from one that wastes your time.

1

Job-specific keyword matching

The AI must extract the actual keywords and required skills from the job posting — not just generic "power words" — and weave them into your resume naturally.

2

Full rewrite, not just suggestions

Highlighting missing keywords and leaving you to edit is only half the job. A great AI tailor should rewrite the resume for you so you can move on.

3

Simultaneous cover letter generation

Your cover letter and resume should reference the same job and feel cohesive. Generating them separately almost always produces a mismatch in tone and emphasis.

4

ATS compatibility

The output must use clean, simple formatting that ATS systems can parse. Fancy tables, graphics, or unusual fonts can cause ATS parsers to reject or misread your resume.

5

Speed — under 60 seconds

You're applying to multiple jobs. A tool that takes 5 minutes per tailoring doesn't scale. The best AI resume tailor should produce results in 30 seconds or less.
